About Us

Our company have flourished as one of the most reliable processors of natural Indian granites in all sizes and colors. The manufacturing and trading unit is based in Jigani Industrial Area, Bangalore (Karnataka).

A well-reputed name in the granite business, Supreme Granites takes pride in emerging as a successful supplier of granites.

Other Locations where we trade

Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Ilkal, Karnataka

Ongale, Srikakulam (Andhra Pradesh)

Warangal, Karimnagar (Telengana)

Kishangarh (Rajasthan)

The strategic and geographical placement of Supreme Granites and its other concerns ensure close proximity and direct access to quarries in South India which are known for the finest and widest varieties of granite

The objective of our establishment is to supply excellent products, in terms of qualitative norms eliminating any nuances that might hinder the progress of the establishment.

Our professionalism, dependability, integrity and dedication to customer satisfaction have earned us a distinguished place in the market, thereby creating exclusivity for our company.
